Welcome to Trash Basketball!

The Rules:
1) Register
2) Shoot your shot
3) Fill Out form
4) Upload score ( highest score uploaded per day is counted towards your total)
5) Dominate the competition ( no cheating )
How to fill out Form:
Linear Tiles : Number of tiles between you and the trash, does not include the tile you are on and the trash can is on.
Offset Tiles : Number of tiles you are offset from the center. Does not include the tile you are on.
Top Net Too? : Select this if your trash went through the top basketball net as well as going in the trashcan
Trick : select the type of shot you took, normal is any shot that does not fall into the other trick shots.
YOU PEOPLE LOVE TO EXPLOIT RULES SO HERE ARE EXTRA RULES. Violators will be DQed
1) Valid trash = Paper towels only no weird objects and no weighting the paper. I'm looking at you alex... banana boy
2) Each piece of trash can only be shot once. If you miss you are not allowed to use that same trash again.
3) Do not generate trash just to shoot.IE using clean papertowel
4) Trick Shots : behind the back must be shot while still standing strait up. Between the legs is like the football long snap position not a granny shot.
Scoring:
1 point per tile from trash. 1/2 point per tile offset from center. That score is then multiplied by the value of 1 + trick multiplier if only hit the trash. Or 2 +trick multipler if you hit both nets. Total value is rounded to nearest tenth
Example: Linear Tiles = 2 , Offset = 2, Top Net = True, Behind the back : (2+2*.5) * 2.5 = 7.5
Examples:
green = inline tiles, blue = offset tiles
